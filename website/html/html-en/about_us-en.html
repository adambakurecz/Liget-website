In May 2023, a new chapter begins in the history of climbing in Veszprém with the opening of Liget Boulder. With 200 square meters of climbing surface and new boulders every week, we offer a challenge for everyone, from beginners to professional climbers. Our terrace directly connected to our gym, spanning over 100 square meters, provides a means for you to connect with nature and exercise in fresh air. If you want to improve quickly and with expert guidance, you can join our training sessions, but thanks to our boulder color-coded system, you will feel comfortable in the gym on your own from the first time. After climbing, you can relax on the Liget terrace with a delicious coffee or a cold beer.

Visit us, and be part of the Liget community!
